These tutorials are designed for absolute beginners to quickly understand and carry out frequently used tasks with Scilab. They are particularly helpful for teachers and students in STEM fields.

The tutorials were developed by the FOSSEE Team at IIT Bombay. You can access the files here: Tutorial Codes and view the companion slides here.

On successful completion, you will be able to:

  • Perform elementary matrix operations
  • Create scientific plots of publication quality
  • Solve algebraic and differential equations

Estimated completion time: 6–8 hours
